BRICKLAYING

Your Story Matters, Join Our Workforce, Join CONSTRUCTION




We offer a variety of Bricklaying courses at Coleg Sir Gâr and Coleg Ceredigion. Whether you’re new to the trade or looking to develop your expertise, our practical training covers everything from basic blockwork and bricklaying techniques to more advanced skills in structural and decorative brickwork.

You’ll gain hands-on experience in measuring, setting out, laying bricks and blocks, and finishing – all within a supportive, industry-standard workshop environment.

These courses open the door to a range of careers in construction, including work as a site bricklayer, craft mason, or opportunities to progress into site supervision, construction management, or self-employment.
